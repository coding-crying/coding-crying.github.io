About Me

AI Researcher, Entrepreneur, and Innovator bridging technology and real-world impact

Who Am I?

I'm an AI researcher and entrepreneur with a BSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Groningen. I'm deeply involved in the push to decentralize AI technologies and contest the consolidation of AI monopolies. I run privacy-focused alternatives to big tech products on my home server and develop open-source AI solutions.

Recently, I architected and developed a cutting-edge language tutoring system powered by conversational speech agents connected to a dynamic graph database model of user memory. Born from personal frustration with traditional language learning, I built this production-ready system in 50 days and released it under an open-source license alongside in-depth research.

My projects span diverse domains: AI music parsers for jam session recordings, cognitive models matched to EEG and MRI data, AI video augmentation for film production, and full sensor fusion architectures for smart appliances. I have experience with venture capitalists, e-commerce sales, and three years living in Europe while earning my AI degree.

Communication is my strongest suit - my curiosity drives me to communicate, and communicating has taught me to trust my intuition. I believe ideas cannot grow to their fullest potential in isolation, and I'm passionate about building technology that serves humanity rather than exploits it.
