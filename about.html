About Me

AI Researcher, Entrepreneur, and Innovator bridging technology and real-world impact

My Journey

I'm an AI researcher and entrepreneur with a BSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Groningen. My multidisciplinary approach combines cutting-edge research, practical engineering, and business acumen to create meaningful technological solutions.

As founder of Silicon Valley Tech Help (SVTH), I built a tech consulting business providing in-home support, network setup, and cybersecurity advisory services. This entrepreneurial experience taught me to translate complex technical concepts into actionable solutions for clients.

My research focuses on conversational AI, social robotics, and machine learning applications. I've developed intelligent systems ranging from agent-based language tutoring platforms to smart kitchen assistants, always with an emphasis on human-centered design and real-world impact.

Beyond software, I bring extensive experience in CAD design, 3D modeling, and rapid prototyping. At Purple Mountain Innovations, I designed casterboard products while consulting on patent prosecution, combining creative design with manufacturing feasibility and legal considerations.
